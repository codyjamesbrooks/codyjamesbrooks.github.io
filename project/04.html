What is Project Euler?

Project Euler is a website comprised of 700+ puzzles that focus on mathematics and computer science. It was birthed in 2001 by Colin Huges and is another great place for math and computer science enthusiasts to go to test and improve their skills.

I just started working on the project Euler puzzles in January 2021, but have taken down the first 30, and am looking forward to the hours that I will get to spend on the remaining 700+!

Similar to the Advent of Code, Project Euler boasts that every problem on its site can be solved in under a minute, using the correct algorithm of course. This is arguably the best part of the puzzles. It is often simple to create a brute force solution but restricting oneself to the time constraint forces a more elegant piece of code.

The hardworking individuals over at Project Euler ask that solutions to problems over #100 not be published outside of their site. In order to comply with this request, I have only posted solutions to the first 30 puzzles on my Github. Feel free to reach out if you would like to ask a question about one of the harder puzzles!
